=== Clause: waveform on voltages and currents on trigger ===

Requirements:
- Up to 10 programmable recordings with different start and stop time
- Provides event recording, waveform capture, waveform display, phasor diagrams, historical trending, time-of-use etc.
- Provides a RS485 and/or TCP/IP communication interface to the metering communication network
- On-board memory up to 8 GB
- Complies with IEC 60255 & 61000 Pt 4 & 6
- Records individual order for even and odd harmonic of voltage and current up to 50th order
