=== Clause: Multi-Function Electronic Meters ===

Requirements (condensed):
- Accuracy classes: ±0.5%
- Current ranges: Accepts current inputs from standard measuring current transformers with rated secondary current of 5A
- Memory / logging capacity: Sufficient built-in memory capable of maintaining all data collected for a minimum of 36 months
- Voltage ranges: 400V/230V
- Communication interface: RS485, RJ45 or similar socket and plug as Approved
- Harmonic order limits: Total Harmonic Distortion (THD): per phase, voltage & current (at least up to 31st harmonic order)
- Frequency range: 50Hz
- Sampling or waveform resolution: Not specified in the text
